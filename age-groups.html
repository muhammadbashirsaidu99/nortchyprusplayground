Age Groups

Below is a list of equipment that is appropriate for each respective age group. Strict adherence to this list is proven to gurantee optimal playground safety.

AGES: 6 – 23 MONTHS
  • Climbing equipment under 32” high
  • Ramps
  • Single file step ladders
  • Slides of modest height
  • Spring rockers
  • Stairways
AGES: 2 YEARS – 5 YEARS
  • Certain climbers
  • Spiral slides up to 360°
  • Spring riders
  • Crawl Tunnels
  • Themed activity panels that promote fantasy play
  • Horizontal ladders
AGES: 5 YEARS - 14 YEARS
  • Ladders – Horizontal, Rung, and Step
  • Overhead rings
  • Merry-go-rounds
  • Spiral slides more than one 360° turn
  • Vertical sliding poles
  • Sports Equipment
