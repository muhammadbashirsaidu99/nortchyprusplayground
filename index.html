Our Goals

Below are the key goals of the Ergonomic and safety evaluation of playgrounds in Lefkosa

Health & Safety

Setting optimum standards that enhance the safety mechanisms of playgrounds so as to achieve better health for all children

Peace of mind

Allow parents to be at peace knowing the safety of their children is of top priority

Inclusiveness

Ensure that there is no barrier related to health and safety when it comes to going to a playground

Accessibility

Make sure that the ploaygrounds become accessible to all by maintaining standard quality assurance

Learn More About Our Work And Our Mission

North Cyprus Playground® is a nonprofit organization working to help families and communities keep kids safe from injuries. Most people are surprised to learn preventable injuries are the #1 killer of kids in the world. Throughout the world, almost one million children die of an injury each year, and almost every one of these tragedies is preventable. North Cyprus Playground aims to reduce traffic injuries, drownings, falls, burns, poisonings and more.

Working together, we can do much more to protect kids. Losing one child is one too many, and we don't want any parent to have to endure the loss of a child. We're calling on everyone to come together, to raise awareness and to get involved. Saving lives is a decision we can all make every day.
